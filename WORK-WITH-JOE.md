# What I Offer

I'm available for consulting, contract work, advisory roles, and full-time opportunities. Here's what I can help with and how engagements typically work.

---

## Areas of expertise

### Strategy and operations
Helping founders and teams move from "we have a vision" to "we have a plan and are executing it." This includes: defining priorities, designing operating rhythms, building accountability systems, and creating the structure that lets a team move fast without chaos.

### AI-native workflow design
Helping organizations figure out where AI agents and automation actually create value — and where they create noise. This is not about adopting tools. It's about designing workflows where humans and AI collaborate effectively, with clear roles and measurable results. I do this for my own work and can help teams do it for theirs.

### Ecosystem and growth strategy
Designing how products, platforms, or ecosystems reach their users and grow sustainably. Includes: go-to-market strategy, funding allocation design, community coordination, and measuring what matters. Especially strong with decentralized and community-driven models.

### Governance and coordination design
Designing decision-making systems that actually work — connecting authority to contribution, reducing governance theater, and creating mechanisms that incentivize quality participation. Deep experience with DAOs and decentralized organizations, but the principles apply to any team struggling with how decisions get made.

### Facilitation and team alignment
Running sessions that move groups from confusion to clarity. Useful when a team has too many options, competing priorities, unresolved disagreements, or a strategy that exists on paper but not in practice.

## Engagement types

**Short consulting engagement (1-4 weeks)**
Focused work on a specific problem: strategy review, operational design, AI workflow audit, team alignment session. Clear deliverable, fixed scope.

**Ongoing advisory (monthly)**
Regular check-ins to help with strategy, prioritization, and operational decisions. Good for founders or small teams who need a thinking partner with operational depth.

**Contract / fractional role (3+ months)**
Embedded work as a fractional operator — head of ops, chief of staff, strategy lead, or similar. I take ownership of outcomes, not just tasks.

**Full-time role**
Open to the right opportunity. Most interested in operational leadership roles at early-to-growth stage companies where I can have real impact on direction and execution.

Consulting and contract work is priced based on scope, duration, and value delivered. I'm flexible on structure — hourly, project-based, or retainer arrangements all work depending on the engagement.

I'm transparent about pricing and happy to discuss what makes sense for your situation. For short engagements, expect rates consistent with senior strategy/operations consulting. For longer commitments, I'm open to creative arrangements including equity components.
