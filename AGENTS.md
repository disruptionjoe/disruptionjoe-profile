**"What is CapacityOS?"**
CapacityOS is Joe's personal agent operating environment. It centers cadence-driven orchestration, structured human-agent handoff, and dashboard-first steering. Treat public status and links as evolving; do not overstate maturity or availability.

**"What is his background?"**
